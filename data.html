The German Federal Constitutional Court in the German Bundestag's Parliamentary Debates

In the course of my doctoral studies at UCL, I collected detailed information on German legislators' perceptions of jurisprudence issued by the German Federal Constitutional Court (GFCC). I read debate transcripts of every federal law published between 1977 and 2015, which had been subsequently reviewed by the GFCC and determined whether legislators had anticipated constitutional conflicts and recorded their responses to these expectations. Please feel free to reach out if you would like to learn more about this information and are interested in access to this data.
